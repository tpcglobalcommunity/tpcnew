TPC Global DAO Mechanism

Transparent and inclusive governance system for the professional trader community

What is DAO?

DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) is an organization run by smart contract code and governed by members through a voting system.

No single authority controls the organization

Decisions are made collectively through voting

Full transparency in all operations

Rules embedded in smart contracts

Participation Mechanism

Members can participate in DAO governance through various ways:

1. Become a Member

Register as a TPC Global member to get voting rights

2. Stake Tokens

Active participation by staking governance tokens

3. Follow Discussions

Engage in proposal discussions and decisions

4. Vote

Use your voting rights to determine the organization's direction

Proposal & Voting

Transparent proposal and voting system for decision making:

Proposal Submission

Members can submit proposals for changes or new initiatives

Discussion Period

Time for discussion and clarification before voting begins

Voting Period

Limited voting period with voting weight based on stake

Implementation

Approved proposals are implemented automatically

Transparency & Accountability

We maintain full transparency in all operational aspects:

On-Chain Governance

All votes and decisions stored on blockchain for public verification

Financial Transparency

Open financial reports auditable by anyone

Decision Log

Complete archive of all decisions and voting results

Regular Reporting

Periodic reports on developments and organizational performance
